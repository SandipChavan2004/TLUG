About TLUG

Our Mission

To promote and support the use of Linux and open-source software within the TKIET community, fostering a culture of collaboration, learning, and innovation.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant community of Linux enthusiasts who contribute to the open-source ecosystem and drive technological innovation in the region.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

TLUG was established with a small group of Linux enthusiasts at TKIET.

2021

First Workshop

Conducted our first Linux installation workshop, helping 50+ students.

2022

Community Growth

Expanded to 100+ active members and organized multiple events.

2023

Open Source Day

Successfully organized the first Open Source Day with 200+ participants.

2024

Current Focus

Expanding our reach and contributing to more open-source projects.

Our Values

Community

Building a strong, supportive community of Linux enthusiasts and open-source contributors.

Learning

Promoting continuous learning and knowledge sharing in the field of open-source technology.

Innovation

Encouraging innovation and creative problem-solving through open-source collaboration.

Collaboration

Fostering a culture of collaboration and mutual support among members.

About TLUG

TLUG is a community-driven initiative at TKIET dedicated to promoting Linux and open-source technologies. We foster learning, collaboration, and innovation among students and professionals.
